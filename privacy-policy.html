Privacy Policy

Introduction

At JSONUtil.com, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle data when you use our tools and services. All processing of data happens locally on your device, and no data is transmitted to our servers or stored by us.

Data Processing

  • All input data is processed directly in your browser.
  • We do not transmit, store, or log your input data on any server.
  • Once you leave or refresh the site, your input data is cleared from the browser.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services, including Google AdSense, to display advertisements on our website. These services may use cookies or similar technologies to deliver personalized ads. These cookies are managed by third parties, and we do not have access to the data collected.

Learn more about how Google uses data here.

Cookies and Analytics

While JSONUtil.com itself does not use cookies or analytics tools, third-party services like Google AdSense may use cookies to deliver relevant ads and analyze site usage.

You can control cookies by adjusting your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect the display of personalized ads but will not impact the functionality of the tools on our site.

Your Data, Your Control

Since no data is transmitted or stored by JSONUtil.com, you retain full control over your input. You can refresh or close the page at any time to clear your data.
